Common Mistakes Businesses Make with WordPress

While WordPress is powerful, flexible and easy to use, many businesses in Kenya unknowingly make decisions that hurt performance, SEO and user experience. At Aminifu Digital Agency, we often encounter underperforming WordPress sites that were built quickly, with little technical planning. These issues are common among businesses attempting DIY or low-cost WordPress development in Kenya without strategic planning or technical oversight.

Here are the most common mistakes we see:

1. Choosing Bloated Themes That Slow Down Load Times

Many off-the-shelf WordPress themes come with unnecessary features, scripts and design elements that arent needed. These slow down your siteleading to poor user experience and lower rankings on search engines. Research shows that a 1-second delay in load time can reduce conversions by 7%, and Google considers speed a key ranking factor.

2. Overusing Drag-and-Drop Builders That Harm SEO

Page builders like Elementor and WPBakery are convenient but often generate bloated code and unnecessary scripts. While theyre useful for prototyping, over-reliance on them can reduce site speed and harm technical SEO. Clean, custom-coded pages load faster and rank better.

3. Installing Insecure Plugins and Ignoring Backups

The WordPress plugin ecosystem is massive, but not all plugins are maintained or secure. Weve seen sites hacked due to outdated or poorly coded plugins. Without regular backups or malware scanning in place, recovery becomes difficult and expensive.

4. Neglecting Technical Optimisation

A fast, SEO-ready WordPress site isnt just about contentit needs schema markup, proper caching, minified scripts, image optimisation, and an XML sitemap. Many sites skip these essentials, which limits their discoverability and visibility on search engines.

5. Poor Mobile Performance

Over 90% of internet users in Kenya access websites via mobile. Yet many WordPress sites are not properly optimised for mobile responsiveness, loading times, or touch usabilityresulting in high bounce rates and lost business.
