    FAQ

    Questions we often get asked

    What services does your digital marketing agency offer?

    We offer a comprehensive range of services including digital marketing, SEO, website development, social media marketing, community management and influencer marketing. Our goal is to help your business grow online achieve its goals and reach its full potential.

    How can digital marketing benefit my business in Kenya?

    Digital marketing can increase your online visibility, attract more customers, and boost sales. It allows you to target specific audiences, track results in real time, and adjust strategies for optimal performance.

    What is SEO, and why is it important for my website?

    SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is the process of optimising your website to rank higher in search engine results. It increases organic traffic, improves visibility, and enhances user experience, leading to more potential customers finding your business without necessarily incurring extra paid advertising costs.

    How long does it take to see results from SEO efforts?

    SEO is a long-term strategy. Typically, it can take 3-6 months to start seeing significant results, depending on the competitiveness of your industry and the current state of your website.

    What is included in your website development service?

    Our website development service includes designing and building a responsive, user-friendly website tailored to your business needs. We also ensure it is optimised for search engines and includes essential features like contact forms, e-commerce capabilities, and more.

    What social media platforms do you manage, and how can they help my business?

    We manage a variety of social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and more. These platforms help increase brand awareness, engage with customers, and drive traffic to your website.

    Can you help with both organic and paid social media strategies?

    Yes, we specialise in both organic and paid social media strategies. We create engaging content for organic growth and develop targeted advertising campaigns to reach a broader audience and achieve specific business goals.

    How do you measure the success of a digital marketing campaign?

    We use various metrics such as website traffic, conversion rates, engagement levels, and return on investment (ROI) to measure the success of a digital marketing campaign. We provide detailed reports to show progress and areas for improvement.

    Can you provide examples of successful campaigns you've managed?

    Yes, we can provide case studies and examples of successful campaigns weve managed. These showcase our expertise and the positive results weve achieved for our clients across various industries.

    What is the process for getting started with your agency?

    Getting started is simple. Contact us to schedule a consultation, during which well discuss your business goals and how we can help. We then create a tailored plan and begin implementing the strategies to achieve your objectives.
